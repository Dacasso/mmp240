Coquito.

A delicious recipe on how to make a Puerto Rican Dessert Drink that is great for the holiday's

Ingredients.

  • 1can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1can evaporated milk
  • 1can evaporated milk
  • 1can creme de coco
  • 1tsp of cinnamon
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • rum, any kind (optional)

In a large pitcher or blender, pour one can of evaporated milk,
One can of sweetened condensed milk,
One can of creme de coco.
Add one tsp of cinnamon and a pinch of nutmeg.
Mix Blend or mix all ingredients really well.
Enjoy. Add rum, if wanted. Drink chilled or with ice. Garnish with cinnamon sticks. Makes 10 servings
