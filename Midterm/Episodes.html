American Horror Story (AHS) is an American horror anthology television series created by Ryan Murphy and Brad Falchuk for the cable network FX. The first installment in the American Story media franchise, seasons of AHS are mostly conceived as self-contained miniseries, following a different set of characters in a new setting within the same fictional universe

Director: Ryan Murphy Cast: Sarah Paulson, Jessica Lange, Zachary Quinto, Emma Roberts, Kim Kardashian & more.

Genre Horror / Sci - Fi
